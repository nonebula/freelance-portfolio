About Me

I began my professional journey leading computing education in UK primary schools, where I fostered a passion for technology and digital skills. This experience guided my transition into web development. After completing a rigorous bootcamp and advanced training at Mercator Academy, I now specialize as a full stack developer/software engineer with a focus on Scala and object-oriented programming.

I strive to create digital experiences that are both beautiful and functional. I believe in writing clean, efficient code and staying up-to-date with the latest industry trends. I got into software engineering to reconnect with my inner child; the lego building, art drawing, playful child I was feels happy when producing beautiful websites.

When I'm not coding, you can find me in the gym or sauna, watching the latest documentary or TV show, or playing with my cats. I've recently began exploring the art of photography, which isn't something I thought I'd ever say! I'm always eager to take on new challenges and collaborate on exciting projects.

Whatever it is you're looking for, I'm an open book and budding software engineer who will give you the conversation you need to take a step towards your desired outcome. Whether that means commissioning me to build your website, or acting as a guide to get you started on your own, I'm more than happy to help.
