About My Government Work

Much of my daily programming work is within the government space. As a result of this, any work is confidential and I am unable to share specific examples or context about the work I have done. However, if you have ever completed a tax return, explored your state pension pages or imported things, chances are you have viewed my work at some point.

Government websites follow fairly strict styling, which is a key reason for me seeking freelance work: to express myself creatively and stay sharp professionally.

I primarily specialise in the frontend space, with strong skills in ensuring accessibility and maximising user experience. I also integrate strong testing practices and work across frontend and backend technologies utilising Scala, Play Framework, MongoDB JavaScript, HTML & CSS.

If you would like to see more specifics of what I can do, contact me for a free consultation. I am upfront about what I can and cannot build, and am only interested in providing the best possible service.
