About Wordpress and Content Management Systems

I have dabbled with Wordpress for years, but last year I took on my first client and redesigned their website. I have since maintained the website and tracked performance free of charge. This is a service I plan to continue for all my customers, always being available for questions or quick fixes should an issue arise.

Content Management Systems (CMS) are highly effective ways for people without explicit coding or programming practice to build, manage and maintain websites or stores without needing to know how to code. However, they can be quite tricky (and expensive).

A key part of my service is providing the tools you need to be able to pick up from where I leave off, meaning no ongoing expenses for you once the website is built, unless you wish to commission me for more features or a redesign. I ensure this by capturing screen recordings and constructing a document containing key links and info along with context specific to your website.

Whether you want a single page or multipage website, I can turnaround a basic Wordpress page within two weeks (or sooner for increased rates), having your website up and running in no time at all.
